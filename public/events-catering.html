What to share when planning

Clear details help the team guide meal quantities, timing, and presentation.

  • Estimated Guests

    Share your total headcount so we can recommend the right portion sizes and avoid waste.

  • Preferred Meal Style

    Let us know if you'd like buffet-style, plated service, or a custom tasting menu for your event.

  • Timing & Duration

    Tell us your event date, start time, and how long you'll need service to keep everything fresh.

  • Venue Context

    Describe the location — whether it's a home, office, or outdoor space — so we can prepare the right setup.

  • Pickup or Delivery

    Discuss whether you'll pick up the order or need delivery, so we can coordinate logistics smoothly.
